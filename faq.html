Frequently Asked Questions

How to make a payment?

To make a payment, you can visit our nearest service center or use our online payment platform. We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfers, and e-wallets.

How to send a package with insurance service?

Sending a package with insurance service is easy. Simply select the insurance option when booking your shipment. You can choose the coverage amount based on the value of your package.

How to report complaints and advice?

We value your feedback. To report complaints or provide advice, you can contact our customer support team through our hotline or email. We will do our best to address your concerns and improve our services.
